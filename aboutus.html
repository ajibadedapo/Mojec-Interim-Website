Established in 1985, MOJEC Group of Companies is an international holdings company headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria with operations in Power, Energy, Technology, Real Estate as well as Mining.

MOJEC International is blazing a trail across the sub-Saharan region with innovave electricity and power soluons. With both local and international partnerships, MOJEC is playing her role in ensuring that she is connually regarded as a leader in the power sector.

MOJEC METER COMPANY

MOJEC Meter Company pioneered the concept of smart metering technology in Nigeria by setting up a state-of-the-art electricity meter plant in the country with a producon capacity of 1,200,000 meters annually designed to serve the local African markets, the EMEA and neighbouring markets; fully equipped to handle customer demand. MOJEC manufactures and supplies different meter types ranging from Single Phase Prepayment Meters, Three Phase Prepayment Meters, Whole Current Meters, LV MD Credit Meter/Prepayment Meters to HT Metering Panel. MOJEC also deploys alongside its meters, data management systems - Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI) and PowerGenie, an online revenue collecon service.

Solaris aims to provide extended power Solar Power Generation and energy storage for residential, industrial and utility clients and also To set the pace as a reputable power provider of alternate power solutions distinguished by innovation and technological advancements
