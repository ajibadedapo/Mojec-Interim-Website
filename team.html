Engr. Wole Solanke (MNSE, COREN) Head, IT/Software

Engr. Solanke Oluwole is the Head IT and Research & Development Manager. He is a proven software specialist and also a visionary that has not hesitated to display his ability to manage projects that steer the company forward on the technological forefront. He's responsible for Mojec International's Automatic meter reading (AMR) system for maximum demand meters and Advanced metering infrastructure (AMI) for Non-MD meters amongst many other R&D projects. Being trained by some of the best meter companies in China on Electrical Meter Assembly and Management, he pioneered the directive to train the first batch of Company Staff that now make up the effective team of factory personnel. This trail of excellence is also evident in his academic career. He's a master's degree holder with an M.Sc. in Computer Science from Babcock University and B.Eng. in Information & Communication Technology from Covenant University. His merits don't end there as he's also a recognized member of the Council for the Regulation of Engineering in Nigeria (COREN), he is also a member of the Nigerian Institute of management and the Nigerian Society of Engineers. Engr. Solanke Oluwole's affinity to always top his best led him to complete a plethora of training courses in Nigeria, Kenya and China. These training courses have polished him into the software specialist, energy meter professional, and competent manager he is today. Since his entry into Mojec International over 8 years ago, Engr. Solanke Oluwole has metamorphosed with Mojec International making him an embodiment of values the company resonates. His knowledge, training and experience has allowed, in part, Mojec International to achieve its industry targets and objectives.
